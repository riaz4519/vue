4.Vuetify Tutorial #4 - Text & Colors

	we can user diffrent class props to style our text .

	-background-color  

	     <p class="red">Red</p>

	     it will make the background color red

	-text color 

		<p class="red--text">Text red</p>

	-backgroud lighten 

		<p class="red lighten-4">Red</p>

	-text darken
